About Me

Gauranga Darshan Das, a disciple of HH Radhanath Swami, is an educator, author, and Dean of the Bhaktivedanta Vidyapitha at ISKCON Govardhan Ecovillage (GEV). With over 35 authored books and 7,500 hours of spiritual discourses, he teaches and writes on Bhakti scriptures like the Bhagavad-gita, Srimad Bhagavatam, and Sri Caitanya-caritamrita. His lucid presentations and systematic guides, including the acclaimed Subodhini series, have been appreciated worldwide.

A former IISc graduate and researcher with 10+ technical publications, he embraced monkhood in 2009, contributing to education, deity worship, and outreach at GEV. He developed a unique 5S curriculum integrating Sadhana, Svadhyaya, Seva, Sadachar, and Sanga. His books, programs, and multimedia teachings continue to inspire children, teens, and adults globally.

My Inspiration

Srila Prabhupada

His Divine Grace A C Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabupada, is the Founder-acharya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON).

HH Radhanath Swami

His Holiness Radhanath Swami is a monk in a Krishna-bhakti tradition and a teacher of Bhakti-yoga. He is a guide, a community builder and activist. He is the author of two New York Times Best-selling books – The Journey Home (a memoir of his search for spiritual truth) and The Journey Within (a contemporary guide to Yoga’s Ancient Wisdom).
